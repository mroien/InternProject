Rating: 4/5An Impressive Novelty

This review is from: Samsung Galaxy Gear 2 Smartwatch (Charcoal Black)

Pros: Purchased for use with my Samsung Galaxy S5, synced up and worked very nicely.

-Can manage the watch face directly on the watch or on the phone using application.
-Helps scratch that mental itch when you "think" you've felt your phone vibrate. With a turn of a wrist see what's up. I'm an Network Administrator and enjoying being able to see immediately what is happening via email alerts
-Vibrates when you disconnect, notifying you that you've left behind your phone (might save you a lost phone).
-Battery lasts for 3 days with normal use.
-Speaker and microphone on watch works well for phone calls on watch. I can hear the phone calls over road noise at 60-75MPH with my hand simply on the steering wheel. Callers could barely tell.
-Standard 22mm wrist band so you can swap out with different bands if you so choose.
-I get a lot of interest and complements from people.
-Matrix watch face is awesome.

Cons: -Way over priced at $449 (as I'm writing this review)
-I didn't end up using half the capabilities after the first week.
-Charging cradle is awkward and can be a pain to get on sometimes, but charges relatively fast.
-It's a little large on my wrist, sometimes I feel like I'm wearing a kids meal watch from Burger King. I find myself taking it off often when typing on keyboards or my laptop.
-Forces you to use the default texting app in order to display text content. Any app can show you alerts, but if you want to get a preview for texts specifically you need to default to the native texting app. I hope I'm just being ignorant on this and this isn't true.

Other Thoughts: I felt myself trying to force myself to like/use it to justify the price spent, which I got on black Friday for $275. Now it's become part of my normal everyday use so it's hard to imagine my workday without it. Only buy if you seriously have an extra couple hundred dollars to just burn. I'll wait to buy another smart watch until they're much lower profile.
